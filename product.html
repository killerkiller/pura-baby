01
🌡️

Warm the water

Use lukewarm water (37–38°C). Never hot. Baby's skin is thinner and more heat-sensitive than adult skin.

02
💧

Dispense & lather

Pump 1–2 presses onto your palm. Work into a gentle lather before applying — avoid direct dispensing on baby's skin.

03
🤲

Wash gently

Using circular motions, cleanse body starting from neck down. Use a separate damp cotton pad for the face.

04
✨

Pat dry & moisturise

Pat (never rub) dry with a soft towel. Apply pūra Moringa Lotion within 3 minutes to lock in moisture.

💡
Pro tip from Dr. Kavitha, Paediatrician
Limit bath time to 5–10 minutes for babies under 12 months. Prolonged water exposure disrupts the skin's natural lipid barrier, especially in winter months when indoor heating is on.
